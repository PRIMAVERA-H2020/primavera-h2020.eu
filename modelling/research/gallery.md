<h2>Model simulation movies</h2>
<h3>Ground-breaking resolution</h3>
<p>The coupled AOGCM version of EC-Earth (NEMO+LIM3 - OASIS - IFS) at  "ground-breaking resolution", i.e. ORCA12 - T1279 (about 16 km in both ocean and atmosphere), is eventually up and running!</p>
<p>One full year of simulation has been completed on our MareNostrum3 supercomputer.<a href="https://youtu.be/JT4ZQAQf5yI"> Watch this YouTube video</a> of a zoom over the North Atlantic of this simulation; it highlights perfectly the influence of cold and warm Gulf Stream eddies on the 2-meter air temperature field (a higher quality version is available <a href="https://www.dropbox.com/s/6y6grhjvgw2t1f3/movie_Montage_ORCA12-T1279_x264_960px_8fps_crf15.mp4?dl=1" title="BSC T1279-ORCA12 movie">here</a>).</p>
<p><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pl5QdYRP8qE">This YouTube video</a> shows the global ultra-high resolution EC-Earth (<a class="yt-simple-endpoint style-scope yt-formatted-string" href="https://www.youtube.com/redirect?q=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.ec-earth.org%2F&amp;v=Pl5QdYRP8qE&amp;event=video_description&amp;redir_token=-p-Adc3JTTSoOZeRNW0VtEc36ON8MTUyODU1MjIyNkAxNTI4NDY1ODI2">http://www.ec-earth.org/</a>) climate model ORCA12-T1279 in action over the North Atlantic. </p>
<h3>Global 10km atmosphere-only simulation</h3>
<p>The HadGEM3-GC3.1 atmosphere model has been run at 10km global resolution for 3 ensemble members over the period 2005-10. An example year of simulations is shown <a href="https://youtu.be/bhfBPrqfV9M">i</a>n <a href="https://youtu.be/bhfBPrqfV9M">this YouTube video</a></p>
<h3>30-year spinup</h3>
<p>The 30-year spinup of the Met Office coupled model HadGEM3-GC3.1-N216ORCA12 with 60-km atmosphere and 1/12 degree ocean resolution was completed following the HighResMIP protocol.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.dropbox.com/s/cm1e2jxjqwgbkt7/orca12_4years_10fps.avi?dl=0" title="Download N216-ORCA12 ocean surface current movie">Watch a short movie of 4 years of this model</a>, comparable to the equivalent <a href="https://www.dropbox.com/s/g7jk2kpmllhht2c/orca025_4years_10fps.avi?dl=0" title="Download N216-ORCA025 ocean surface current">1/4 degree ocean model</a><span class="ext-link">. (Note: click on the link to download each movie via Dropbox). These movies show the daily surface ocean current speed - the flickering comes from atmospheric storms passing over the ocean. Now <a href="https://twitter.com/MetOffice_Sci/status/880427472092897284" title="N216-ORCA12 movie of daily surface ocean currents">available on YouTube too</a>. <br/></span></p>
<p><span class="ext-link"></span>These were made using the <a href="https://www.blender.org/" title="Blender software package">Blender software package</a>.</p>
